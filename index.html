iPhone

iPhone is a line of smartphones produced by Apple Inc. that use
Apple's own iOS mobile operating system. The first-generation
iPhone was announced by then-Apple CEO Steve Jobs on January
9, 2007. Since then, Apple has annually released new iPhone
models and iOS updates. As of November 1, 2018, more than
2.2 billion iPhones had been sold. As of 2022, the iPhone accounts
for 15.6% of global smartphone market share.[3]
